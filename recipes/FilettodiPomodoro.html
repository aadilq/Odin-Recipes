• 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/4 pound of thinly sliced Prosciutto Ham
  • 1 cup of julienned Onion
  • 2 jars of Homemade Marinara Sauce
  • 1 and half pounds of Penne Rigate
  • Crushed Peppers
  • Pecorino Romano Cheese
  • Fresh Basil
  1. With each piece of prosciutto, roll each piece and trim it into 1/4 pieces

  2. In a hot pan, combine olive oil, garlic, onions, and prosciutto. Cook until the onions become translucent and the prosciutto becomes crispy.

  3. Add Marinara sauce to the mix and stir

  4. Add fresh crushed black peppers when the sauce begins to simmer

  5. After the crushed black peppers, add the Pecorino Romano cheese and fresh basil leaves.

  6. Lastly, pour the cooked pasta into the pan with the sauce and voila, Filetto di Pomodoro
