• 1/2 Pound of Ground beef
  • 1 large Onion
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • 2 cans of Tomato Sauce
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of dried Oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of crushed dried Rosemary
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon of pepper
  • 2 cups of mashed potato flakes
  • 1-1/2 cups of boiling water
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1-1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
    1. In a large saucepan, cook the groundbeef, onions, and garlic over a medium heat.

    2. In the pan with the ground beef, onion, and garlic, stir in the tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, and seasonings. Have it sit for 15-20 minutes.

    3. In a large bowl with the potato flakes, stir in boiling water until it is blended. After stir in eggs, add flour and salt and stir until it is all combined.

    4. Roll into 3/4-in.-thick ropes; cut ropes into 3/4-in. pieces.

    5. Bring a large saucepan of water to a boil. Cook gnocchi in batches for 30-60 seconds or until gnocchi float. Remove with a slotted spoon. Place in a large bowl; top with sauce.
