  1. Cut up some onions and garlic

  2. Add a pack of Spaghetti Noodles to a large pot of salted water and cook until they are firm

  3. Pour two tablespoons of olive oil in a medium cooking pot over a heat that is medium high

  4. To that same pot with the two tablespoons of olive oil, add ground beef and stir well to cook for about 5-7 minutes

  5. After the ground beef cooked from 5-7 minutes, add the cut up onions and garlic from before.

  6. Add the tomato Sauce, tomato paste, worcestershire sauce, sugar, salt, and paper.

  7. Turn the heat down to a low and let the meat sauce simmer for 10 minutes uncovered.

  8. Add your spaghetti to the meat sauce and voila, Spaghetti Bologanese
